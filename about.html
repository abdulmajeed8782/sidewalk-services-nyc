Who We Are

Eden Contracting NY is a leading specialist in NYC sidewalk repair, dedicated to resolving DOT violations and addressing all types of concrete issues across all five boroughs. With a deep understanding of the unique challenges posed by New York City's infrastructure, our team of certified and experienced professionals provides comprehensive solutions for Manhattan, Brooklyn, Queens, the Bronx, and Staten Island.

We are committed to delivering high-quality, durable, and compliant sidewalk services that restore safety and value to your property. Our expertise encompasses everything from meticulous repairs and new installations to ensuring full DOT compliance and efficient violation removal.
