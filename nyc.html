NYC Sidewalk Repair & Maintenance

At Eden Contracting NY, we are dedicated to providing comprehensive sidewalk repair and maintenance solutions across all five boroughs of New York City. Our deep understanding of local regulations, combined with years of hands-on experience, ensures that every repair and maintenance service we undertake is not only durable but also fully compliant with NYC DOT standards. Common Sidewalk Issues in NYC and Our Repair Solutions

Cracked and Uneven Slabs: These are the most frequent causes of tripping hazards and DOT violations. We employ precise techniques to repair or replace damaged sections, ensuring a smooth and level surface.

Tree Root Damage: Protruding tree roots are a common culprit behind sidewalk damage in NYC. Our approach involves careful root pruning and the installation of root barriers, always in compliance with city regulations to protect both the sidewalk and the tree.

Collapsed Sidewalk Sections: Often indicative of underlying structural issues or water damage, collapsed sections require immediate attention. We conduct thorough assessments to identify the root cause and implement robust repair strategies.

DOT Violation Remediation: If you've received a DOT violation notice, we provide comprehensive services to address all cited issues, from minor defects to major structural concerns, ensuring your sidewalk passes re-inspection.
